Zachary Nightingale

I am a student, designer, and aspiring creative from Northern California! My current interests
cover are wide range of activities from producing indie pop music, streaming and posting
YouTube videos about Super Smash Bros Melee, learning new recipes to cook, and
enjoying my part-time job as a barista at a local coffee shop. Sometimes I struggle to
find enough time to endulge in all of my favorite parts of life, but I have to remember
that as long as I am doing what makes me happy, it's okay to take it slow. In school I am
hoping to find a career that lets me be creative and genuinly happy while also being able
to get by. That has led me to the Design program, where I am becoming especially interested
in design history and user experience. I know I have a lot going on, but I'm excited to see
where the field will take me and what the next big part of my life will be!
